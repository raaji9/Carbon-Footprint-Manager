Carbon Footprint Manager

About This Project

The Carbon Footprint Manager is a tool designed to help individuals calculate and understand their personal carbon emissions based on daily activities such as transportation, electricity usage, diet, and waste management. By providing relevant data, users can estimate their annual carbon footprint and receive personalized recommendations on how to reduce their environmental impact.

How It Works

  • Choose your country from the dropdown menu to apply country-specific emission factors.
  • Adjust the sliders to match your daily or weekly activities.
  • Click the "Calculate CO2 Emissions" button to see your results and recommendations.
  • You can also check and download the history of your calculations.

Why It Matters

Reducing your carbon footprint is crucial for mitigating climate change. By understanding the sources of your emissions, you can make informed decisions to lower your impact on the environment. Small changes in daily habits can lead to significant reductions in carbon emissions, contributing to global sustainability efforts.
